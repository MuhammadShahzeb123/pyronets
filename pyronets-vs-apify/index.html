Why Choose Pyronets Over Apify?

Apify is a powerful platform for developers who want to build and run their own scraping workflows. Its marketplace of pre-built Actors can save time, but you still need technical expertise to configure, maintain, and troubleshoot them. Costs can also escalate quickly with compute-unit pricing.

Pyronets takes a fundamentally different approach: we're a service, not a platform. You tell us what data you need, and our expert team builds custom crawlers, manages anti-bot bypass, validates data quality, and delivers clean results on your schedule. No compute units, no Actor debugging, no infrastructure management.

When Apify Makes Sense

Apify is ideal if you have developers who enjoy building scraping workflows, need a platform to host and schedule your own scrapers, want access to a marketplace of community-built tools, and prefer a self-service approach with more hands-on control.
