Why ticketing data is the hardest to scrape.

That's where Pyronets comes in. Our team of reverse engineers and data architects build custom crawlers from the ground up — specifically designed to navigate these defenses ethically and reliably, delivering the data you need without interruption.

🛡 Anti-Bot Expertise

How we bypass the toughest defenses

Ticketing platforms invest millions in anti-bot technology. Here's how Pyronets stays ahead.

🛡 Akamai Bot Manager

Used by Ticketmaster, AXS, and many primary platforms. Akamai uses sensor data collection, device fingerprinting, and behavioral biometrics. Our crawlers generate authentic sensor payloads, rotate device profiles, and simulate human-like interaction patterns to maintain access.

☁ Cloudflare Turnstile

Growing presence on mid-tier ticketing platforms. We handle Cloudflare's JavaScript challenges, Turnstile widgets, and managed rules through purpose-built challenge solvers that operate in real-time without manual intervention.

🛡 PerimeterX (HUMAN)

Deployed by StubHub and other resale markets. PerimeterX monitors JavaScript execution patterns, WebGL fingerprints, and mouse movement heuristics. Our crawlers mirror genuine browser environments with full DOM interaction capabilities.

💥 DataDome

Used by European ticketing platforms. DataDome analyzes request velocity, header ordering, and TLS handshake characteristics. We counter with residential proxy rotation, JA3 fingerprint management, and request throttling algorithms.

❓ FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Can you really scrape Ticketmaster?

Yes. Ticketmaster is protected by Akamai Bot Manager, which is one of the most advanced anti-bot solutions. Our custom crawlers are specifically engineered to navigate Akamai's sensor data, device attestation, and behavioral challenges. We deliver real-time pricing and availability data from Ticketmaster reliably.

How do you handle CAPTCHAs?

Our infrastructure includes integrated CAPTCHA solving for reCAPTCHA v2/v3, hCaptcha, Cloudflare Turnstile, and custom challenge-response systems. Solutions are generated in real-time without manual intervention, ensuring uninterrupted data streams.

How frequently can data be updated?

Depending on the platform and your requirements, we can refresh data as frequently as every 30 seconds. For most use cases, we recommend 1-5 minute intervals to balance data freshness with cost efficiency.

Do you support historical data?

Yes. We maintain historical price, availability, and listing data going back based on when you start the project. This enables price trend analysis, demand forecasting, and retrospective market research.

Is your scraping legal?

We operate within legal frameworks and follow ethical scraping practices. We collect publicly accessible data only and comply with relevant data protection regulations. Our legal team can provide guidance specific to your use case.

What format is the data delivered in?

We deliver clean, structured data in the format that works best for you — JSON via REST API, CSV/Excel exports, webhooks for real-time feeds, direct database insertion (PostgreSQL, MongoDB, BigQuery), or S3/GCS drops.
